Row 2 — Profile

What I actually do

Most of my work starts the same way: a business already has a process — a notebook, a messy spreadsheet, a chat thread full of order updates — and just needs it turned into something fast, reliable, and easy for staff to use on a phone.

I build that as a single, self-contained web app with a Google Sheet as its database. Your data lives in your own Google account from day one, not on a server I control, and the app installs like a normal app on Android phones — even on a so-so connection.

You own the data

Everything runs on your own Google Sheet and Google account — not a third-party database you don't control.

Built for your team

Simple screens and big buttons for staff who aren't technical, with separate access for owners vs. cashiers or encoders.

Works on what you have

Installs like an app on Android phones and tablets, and keeps working even when the internet doesn't cooperate.
